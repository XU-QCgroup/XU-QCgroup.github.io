Opportunities

We welcome inquiries from motivated researchers interested in quantum chemistry, computational materials science, molecular simulation, and related areas.

Prospective Postdoctoral Researchers

Prospective postdoctoral researchers whose interests align with the group’s research directions are welcome to contact us. Please include a CV, a brief description of research interests, representative publications, and possible start date.

Prospective Graduate Students

Prospective graduate students should apply through the Iowa State University Department of Chemistry graduate program . Students interested in joining the XU-QCgroup are encouraged to mention their interest in computational chemistry, quantum chemistry, molecular simulation, and/or computational materials research in their application.

Undergraduate Research

Undergraduate students interested in research experience are welcome to contact us. Please include your major, year, relevant coursework or programming experience, research interests, and expected time availability.

Visiting Scholars and Students

We also welcome inquiries from visiting scholars and visiting students whose research interests align with the group’s activities.
