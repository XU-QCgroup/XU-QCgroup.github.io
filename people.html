Research interests: Theoretical chemistry, electronic structure theory, non-adiabatic molecular dynamics, photochemistry, and environmental modelling.

Connor is an undergraduate student interested in theoretical chemistry, physics beyond the Standard Model, and electronic structure theory. His current projects include developing a spherical tensor formalism for dispersion in the EFP framework and computationally studying methane combustion pathways.

Email: cfundell@iastate.edu

Fun detail: Connor enjoys stargazing, hiking, and music. He has visited more than ten U.S. National Parks, including Zion and Mammoth Cave.
