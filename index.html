Quantum chemistry and computational materials research

The XU-QCgroup develops and applies quantum chemistry, computational materials science, and molecular simulation methods to understand complex chemical and materials systems.

Research

Our research focuses on computational approaches for understanding chemical bonding, electronic structure, molecular interactions, and structure-property relationships in materials and chemical systems.
